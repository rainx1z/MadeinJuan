Refund & Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Please review our policy below.

Return Policy

Key Points:

  • 30-day return policy from date of delivery
  • Items must be unused, in original packaging with tags attached
  • Proof of purchase required

How to Return an Item:

  1. Initiate your return: Log in to your account and visit the "My Orders" section to start your return.
  2. Package your item: Include all original packaging, accessories, and tags. We recommend using the original shipping box if possible.
  3. Ship your return: Use the prepaid return label provided in your return confirmation email. Drop off at any authorized shipping center.

Note: Some items are final sale and cannot be returned, including clearance items, personalized products, and intimate apparel.

Return Shipping:

We provide free return shipping for all eligible returns within the continental US. International returns are subject to shipping fees.

Refund Policy

Standard Refunds

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Store Credit

You may opt for store credit instead of a refund, which will be issued immediately and comes with a 10% bonus credit.

Refund Timeline:

Stage Timeframe Details
Return Processing 1-3 business days After we receive your return
Refund Issued Immediately To original payment method
Funds Available 3-10 business days Depends on your bank/credit card

Non-refundable items: Gift cards, downloadable software products, and personalized items cannot be refunded.

Exchange Policy

How to Exchange an Item:

We're happy to exchange items within 30 days of delivery for a different size, color, or style (subject to availability).

Exchange Process:

  1. Contact our customer service team to initiate an exchange
  2. We'll place the new item on hold while you return the original
  3. Once we receive your return, we'll ship the exchanged item

Exchange Shipping:

Standard exchange shipping is free. Expedited shipping options are available for an additional fee.

Price Differences:

If the new item costs more, you'll pay the difference. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference.

Frequently Asked Questions

If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact our customer service team immediately. We'll arrange for a free return and send you a replacement at no additional cost.
