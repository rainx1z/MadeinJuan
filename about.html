Our Story

Made in Juan is a platform dedicated to celebrating the rich heritage of traditional Filipino cuisine. We are committed to preserving authentic recipes and sharing the unique flavors and stories that define Filipino culture.

Mission

Our mission is to honor tradition, promote cultural pride, and inspire a deeper appreciation for Filipino food both locally and globally. Here in Made in Juan, we believe that every dish carries history, identity, and a sense of community. Through our work, we aim to keep these traditions alive and relevant for generations to come.

Launch

December 2020 - Made in Juan officially launched with just 6 products from local artisans and small businesses.

First 1,000 Customers

March 2021 - We celebrated reaching our first major milestone with 1,000 satisfied customers.

Sustainability Award

October 2022 - Recognized as "Most Sustainable E-Commerce Platform" by the Green Business Council.
